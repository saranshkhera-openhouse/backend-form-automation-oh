CP Inventory Form
  1. Please submit all leads through this form only and not over WhatsApp etc.
  2. It is mandatory to be onboarded as a Channel Partner before submitting any inventory. Please get in touch with the OH Team to get onboarded.
  3. We will require 72 working hours to revert with the next steps.
  4. If the property is relevant and as per our requirement, we will schedule a visit to the apartment.
  5. Once visited, the apartment will be tagged against the CP code in the system.
  6. In case the same property is being presented by two different CPs, priority will be given to the CP with earlier submission. We therefore encourage you to send us inventory immediately to claim your priority.
  7. All tagged inventory will remain exclusive to the CP until 30 days from the date of property visit or 15 days from the date of owner meeting, whichever is earlier.
  8. In case of disputes, Openhouse will strive to preserve the interests of the Channel Partner for long-term engagement but the decision of the Openhouse team will be absolute in this regard.
